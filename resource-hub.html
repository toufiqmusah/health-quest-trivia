Quick Facts

  • What is HIV Self-Testing (HIVST)? HIVST lets you collect your own specimen, perform the test, and read the result privately. Reactive tests must be confirmed at a clinic.
  • What is PrEP? Pre-exposure prophylaxis (PrEP) is HIV prevention medicine for HIV-negative people at higher risk.
  • Privacy & Confidentiality: You can test in private and choose who to tell. Health workers must keep your information confidential.

Have questions? Reach out to your local health provider or visit trusted health websites for more information.
